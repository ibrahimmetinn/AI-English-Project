Simple Past
Completed actions in the past. Uses V2 form (went, played). Questions use "did".
Geçmiş Zaman (Simple Past)
Bitmiş geçmiş eylemler için kullanılır. Olumlu cümlede fiil 2. haline (V2) gelir: go→went, play→played. Olumsuz ve sorularda did/didn't gelir ve fiil yalın hale döner.

✦ She went to school.
✦ Did she go?
Present Simple & Continuous
Simple = habits & routines. Continuous = actions happening right now (is doing).
Şimdiki Zamanlar
Present Simple: Alışkanlıklar ve genel gerçekler için. Anahtar kelimeler: always, usually, every day.
Present Continuous: Şu an devam eden eylemler. Anahtar kelimeler: now, at the moment, right now + is/are + V-ing.

✦ She drinks coffee every morning. (alışkanlık)
✦ She is drinking coffee now. (şu an)
Future: Will & Be going to
Will = instant decisions & promises. Be going to = plans & predictions with evidence.
Gelecek Zaman
Will: Konuşma anında verilen ani kararlar, söz vermek, tahmin. "Şimdi karar verdim!"
Be going to: Önceden planlanmış eylemler veya görsel kanıta dayalı tahminler. "Bakıyorum ve görüyorum ki..."

✦ I'll help you! (ani karar → will)
✦ I'm going to visit Paris. (bilet aldı → be going to)
Question Tags
Short questions at the end. Positive sentence → negative tag, and vice versa.
Eklenti Soruları
Cümle olumlu (+) ise tag olumsuz (-), cümle olumsuz (-) ise tag olumlu (+) olur.
İstisna: "I am..." cümlelerinin tag'i "aren't I?" olur.
Emir cümlesinin tag'i: "will you?"

✦ She is late, isn't she?
✦ You don't know, do you?
✦ I am right, aren't I?
So / Neither / Too / Either
"Me too" in English. Positive: So do I / I do too. Negative: Neither do I / I don't either.
"Ben de" Yapısı
Olumlu "Ben de": So + yardımcı + özne (So do I) veya özne + yardımcı + too (I do, too).
Olumsuz "Ben de": Neither + yardımcı + özne (Neither do I) veya özne + yardımcı(olumsuz) + either (I don't, either).

✦ A: I love this. B: So do I. / I do, too.
✦ A: I can't swim. B: Neither can I.
Comparatives & Superlatives
Two things: bigger than / more expensive than. Best of all: the biggest / the most.
Karşılaştırmalar
Comparative (iki şey): Kısa sıfat → -er than (bigger than). Uzun sıfat → more ... than (more expensive than).
Superlative (en üstün): Kısa sıfat → the ...-est (the fastest). Uzun sıfat → the most ... (the most beautiful).

✦ My bag is heavier than yours.
✦ This is the most difficult test.
Pronouns
Possessive adjective: my car. Possessive pronoun: it's mine. Reflexive: himself.
Zamirler
Possessive Adjective (my, your, his...): Yanına mutlaka isim ister → my car, her bag.
Possessive Pronoun (mine, yours, hers...): İsim istemez, ismin yerini tutar → It's mine.
Reflexive (myself, himself...): Özne ve nesne aynı kişi → She hurt herself.

✦ This is my book. / This book is mine.
Quantifiers
Much/a little = uncountable. Many/a few = countable. Few/little = not enough.
Miktar Belirleyiciler
Many / a few: Sayılabilir çoğullar (books, eggs).
Much / a little: Sayılamayanlar (water, money, time).
A few / A little: Az ama yeterli (olumlu anlam).
Few / Little: Az ve yetersiz (olumsuz anlam).

Either / Neither (İki Seçenek)
Either: İkisinden herhangi biri olur → olumlu/kabul. Take either one.
Neither: İkisi de değil → olumsuz/ret. Neither option worked.
Both: İkisi birden → olumlu/toplam. Both books were great.

✦ A: Tea or coffee? B: Either — I don't mind.
✦ Neither restaurant was open — both were closed.
✦ I read both books and loved them.
Vocabulary — Unit 4
Key words: evidence, cautious, sarcastic, appropriate, globalize. Used in context.
Kelimeler
Evidence (isim): kanıt, delil → strong evidence
Cautious (sıfat): dikkatli, ihtiyatlı → be cautious
Sarcastic (sıfat): alaycı → a sarcastic tone
Appropriate (sıfat): uygun → appropriate behaviour
Globalize (fiil): küreselleştirmek → to globalize culture
